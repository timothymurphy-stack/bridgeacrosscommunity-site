The Charity That Is Not Spent

A perpetual, safeguarded fund — placed within a Credit Union — that is activated for members and restored via everyday transactions and community savings.

Principles

  • Perpetual: The fund is never spent, only activated and restored.
  • Local: Rooted in Cornwall to begin, serving first families with dignity.
  • Measurable: Progress tracked via Community One’s Milestones.

Cycle (Simplified)

  1. Fund placed with Credit Union.
  2. Members onboarded through Community One.
  3. Transactions + savings restore the fund.
  4. Impact reported; next milestone unlocked.
