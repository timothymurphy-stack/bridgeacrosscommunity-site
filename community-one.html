Community One — The 7 Milestones

From one user to five hundred — a disciplined, loving sequence that lights Cornwall’s first families and proves the model.

The Milestones

  1. Milestone 1: 1 user
  2. Milestone 1.5: 6 users (and switch on the software)
  3. Milestone 2: 10 users
  4. Milestone 3: 20 users
  5. Milestone 4: 50 users
  6. Milestone 5: 100 users
  7. Milestone 6: 200 users
  8. Milestone 7: 500 users
Each milestone is a quiet proof point. We move only when stewardship, member care, and data quality are excellent.
