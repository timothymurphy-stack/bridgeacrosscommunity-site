Replication Outline

Cornwall Community One → a few → regional (not national first)

Principles

  • Quiet proof before noise: Community One first, then a handful, then regional.
  • Same pattern, same dignity: restoration loops, daily visibility, privacy compact.
  • Human cadence: Chair + Steward + Practitioner — the three-in-concert.

Community One (Cornwall)

  • Light Saver 1; confirm daily restoration cadence; balances visible by night.
  • Reach ten, then fifty households; document evidence and variance.
  • Achieve 100 → 200 → 350 → 500 with audit-ready notes.

Next few communities

  • Replicate with the same rails; local partners anchor trust (credit unions, retailers).
  • Share a compact replication pack: roles, cadence, privacy, retailer batch template.
  • Maintain daily restorations early; shift cadence only when habit is established.

Regional readiness

  • Operational reliability: nightly batch stable; anomalies handled within 24h.
  • Governance: privacy compact lived; data minimised; narratives respectful.
  • Metrics: restoration latency trending down; habit persistence trending up.

Signals to proceed

  • Chair stance: remains at Saver 1 level even as numbers grow.
  • Steward cadence: weekly steady; board notes concise and cumulative.
  • Community trust: savers refer savers; retailers expand coverage.

In one line: Prove the pattern in Cornwall, replicate quietly, then scale regionally — protecting dignity at each step.
