Thesis

CSS + BAC form the dignity rail. On top, an ethical Sales Promotions Agency (SPA) compounds value by directing demand, lowering the cost-of-need, and funding restoration — without spending the corpus.

Revenue Motions (Illustrative)

  • Retailer participation: SAIV-linked promotions that share value back to restoration and community partners.
  • Affinity campaigns: church/employer cohorts where sponsors underwrite starts and celebrate loops completed.
  • Procurement savings: channel aggregate purchasing to reduce essential costs; share verified savings as revenue.
  • Data-light insights: product innovation for Credit Unions arises from patterns of real-time saving, not personal dossiers.

Guardrails

  • Dignity first: no exploitation; no dark patterns; no selling of personal data.
  • Transparency: every promotion shows how value flows to restoration/community.
  • No corpus risk: SPA funds activity from partner value-share and efficiencies, not from the never-spent corpus.

Why It Matters

Real-time discretionary saving creates new, compounding surfaces for value creation. Over months and quarters, the profile of activity becomes profound — enabling partners to design products that honour habit, reduce cost-of-need, and strengthen the restoration cadence across communities.
