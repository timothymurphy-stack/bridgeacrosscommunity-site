Governance & Privacy Compact

Private — invitation only

Purpose

Protect dignity, protect the corpus, and protect truth. Governance exists to keep faith with savers and investors: consent before data, oversight before scale, privacy above spectacle.

Principles

  • Minimal data: collect only what’s necessary to serve and safeguard.
  • Explicit consent: clear, human-readable consent for placements and restoration cadence.
  • Access control: invite-only portal; least privilege; short session duration.
  • Independent oversight: quarterly review; raise & resolve issues; publish a quiet internal note.
  • Auditability without theatre: logs available to oversight; never public performance.
  • Right to be quiet: opt-out of publicity; communications by permission only.

Operational Safeguards

  • Never-spent corpus: funds are placed and restored; corpus remains intact.
  • Eligibility & verification: practitioner-led checks; documented but humane.
  • Incident handling: pause, assess, correct; communicate quietly to oversight within 72 hours.
  • Data retention: keep for the minimal period; encrypt at rest; purge on schedule.

Reporting Rhythm

  • Weekly: Chair + steward + practitioner one-page view (consent, placements, restorations, issues).
  • Monthly: restoration rate, households in cadence, issues resolved.
  • Quarterly: oversight sign-off; privacy checks passed; recommendations tracked.
