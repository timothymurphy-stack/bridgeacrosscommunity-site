First Light — Activation Note

Quiet ignition in Community One — daily habit, daily evidence

Purpose

Light the first household in Cornwall quietly, with fierce intent. Establish the first restoration loop, visible by day’s end, and confirm that the cadence is humane and reliable.

People & roles

  • Chair: stands at Saver 1; protects dignity; holds the cadence.
  • Steward: consent, privacy compact, baseline; monitors daily evidence.
  • Practitioner: supports household, guides the first week flow.

Sequence (Day 0 → Day 7)

  • Day 0: Consent captured; privacy compact affirmed; baseline noted.
  • Day 1: First discretionary amount captured; balance visible by night.
  • Days 2–3: Additional small amounts; nightly restorations; cadence adjusted if needed.
  • Day 4–6: Practitioner check-ins; anomalies flagged; narrative recorded.
  • Day 7: First loop confirmed; note logged; household remains supported.

Cadence & evidence

  • Daily restorations initially — fast habit formation, early proof.
  • Retailer batch ingested nightly; reconciliation noted by steward.
  • Narrative dignity preserved; data minimised; board-ready notes kept.

Exit of First Light

  • KR1.1 Consent + privacy compact on file; baseline captured.
  • KR1.2 First advance placed; cadence co-agreed.
  • KR1.3 First restoration loop completed and logged.

Signal: First Light is not a pilot; it is the careful ignition of a permanent, community-owned capability.
