The Gap

For decades, mainstream rails optimised for spend and real-time depletion. The financially excluded experienced volatility without the counterweight of structured accumulation. Advice was offered without an infrastructure capable of holding the change.

The Answer

CSS — Community Savings Solution is a safeguarded, never-spent savings architecture that restores cadence, credibility, and consent. It treats savings as a first-class product — built to hold steady under real life.

The Bridge

BAC — Bridge Across Community is the distribution and onboarding channel into churches, employers, and community organisations. It’s where Cornwall’s Community ONE begins — quietly, locally, and repeatably.

First Light

The Chairperson stands at the level of Saver One — the first family — quietly but with fierce intent to see them begin. There is no time clock; the act is authorship. Momentum starts with consent, safeguards, and a first advance that proves a restoration loop is possible.

Finite Milestones → Replication

M1 → consent & safeguards. M1.5 → first advance. M2 → restoration cadence. Habit formation follows. Cornwall becomes a living template: Community ONE by stealth, then a few more communities, then regional; national later.

Bridge Across Community · CSS is the infrastructure; BAC is its bridge into community.
