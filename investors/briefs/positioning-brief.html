Genesis: The Call That Went Unanswered

For over two decades, leaders have called for innovation to reach the financially excluded. In 2001, Ron Sandler’s landmark review demanded new models for those left behind. Years later, during the WONGA crisis, Archbishop Justin Welby challenged the financial system to act in favour of the poorest. And yet, the gap remained.

Enter CSS and BAC

CSS — Community Savings Solution builds the infrastructure of accumulation where the status quo has engineered depletion. BAC — Bridge Across Community is the distribution and onboarding channel through churches, employers, and community organisations. Cornwall’s Community ONE is the template for replication.

The First Light

John Doerr’s OKR philosophy recognises that every great system begins with a single defining action. For us, that action is the Chairperson of Heart standing at the level of Saver One — the first family — quietly, with fierce intent to see them begin. That act authors the cadence of credibility.

Finite, Defining Milestones

Momentum becomes observable and trusted early: M1 → consent & safeguards; M1.5 → first advance; M2 → restoration cadence demonstrated; onward to habit formation. One household becomes ten, then fifty, then five hundred. Cornwall proves; replication follows.

Bridge Across Community · CSS is the infrastructure; BAC is its bridge into community.
