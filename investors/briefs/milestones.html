The 7 Milestones

From Saver 1 to 500 — finite, auditable, human

M1 — The First Saver (Family One)

Objective: Light the first household quietly, with fierce intent. Steward captures pre-baseline; practitioner supports first-week flow.

  • KR1.1 Household consent + privacy compact acknowledged.
  • KR1.2 First advance placed; restoration schedule co-agreed (short daily intervals to build habit).
  • KR1.3 First restoration loop completed and logged.

M2 — Ten Savers

Evidence of pattern: small, real-time discretionary amounts captured and restored. Steward notes variance; practitioner cadence adjusted.

M3 — Fifty Savers

Operational reliability across multiple retailers. Daily restorations continue (end-of-day batch) with visible balances by night.

M4 — One Hundred

Community narrative established; privacy compact affirmed. First local replication candidate earmarked.

M5 — Two Hundred

Chair + Steward + Practitioner working as a three. Ratio evidence improves; restoration latency documented.

M6 — Three Hundred & Fifty

Retailer coverage widened; promotions data ready for SPA exploration (without compromising dignity-first stance).

M7 — Five Hundred

Audited run. Board-ready metrics; replication pack finalised for next communities (Cornwall first, then a handful, then regional).

Short intervals early: daily restorations establish habit and produce evidence quickly. Balances visible by the end of each day; retailer settlements may follow their normal cycle.
